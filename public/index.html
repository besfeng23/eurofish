Imported flowers

Flower buyers need dates, varieties, condition, and deadline control.

For florists, decorators, wholesalers, and event suppliers, the inquiry should include flower type, color palette, quantity, event date, receiving location, and substitute options.

Premium roses

Quicksand, Toffee, Playa Blanca, and other soft neutral or event-grade options, subject to current availability.

Orchids & tropicals

Mokara, Vanda, Phalaenopsis, Anthurium, Monstera, Protea, and structural event selections, subject to shipment timing.

Wholesale controls

Stem count, box quantity, grade, cut-off time, cold-chain expectations, and damage-claim rules should be clarified before quotation.
