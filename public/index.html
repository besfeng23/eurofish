Company Profile

Reliable Milkfish Fry and Premium Perishable Imports

1064 Euro-Fish Trades is a Philippine-based trading and importation company specializing in milkfish fry, ornamental plants, and selected cut flowers. Operating from Putatan, Muntinlupa City, the company serves aquaculture operators, traders, nurseries, florists, event suppliers, and commercial buyers that require reliable sourcing, proper documentation, and fast-moving logistics for sensitive biological and perishable goods.

The company is publicly listed in government importer records under the name 1064 Euro-Fish Trading. Its work is centered on regulated, time-sensitive product categories where compliance, coordination, and careful handling are essential.
